Membership Level Details

Non-Profit membership

  • Annual fees: Free of charge (FOC)
  • For non-profit organizations only
  • Proof of non-profit status required
  • All membership benefits

Bronze membership

  • Annual fees: US$500
  • Automatic subscription to Chamber's update emails and monthly Newsletter
  • Attendance at Chamber's city monthly meetings
  • 25% discount of local seminars and workshops

Silver membership

  • Annual fees: US$750
  • All privileges of Bronze except as noted here
  • 35% discount of local seminars and workshops
  • 20% discount of state seminars and workshops

Gold Membership

  • Annual fees: US$1000
  • All privileges of Bronze except as noted here
  • 50% discount of local seminars and workshops
  • 40% discount of state seminars and workshops
